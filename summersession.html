Summer Session

The Graphic Studio was recruited to refresh the UCSD Summer Session brand.
I create content and write copy that bridges the gap between Summer Session and students.

ROLE AS Public Relations Coordinator

TEAM Designer: Scott Liu

TIMEFRAME February 2020 - Present

OBJECTIVE

Increase enrollment for UC San Diego Summer Session 2020 with a focus on continuing students.

What I Did

In response to remote classes, I pivoted our campaign from "Experience Summer in San Diego" to "Make the Most of Your Summer".

To provide students remote resources, I set up a Reddit AMA, Instagram FAQ's, prepared department graphics, created newsletters, & moderated giveaways.



RESULTS

Enrollment increased by 47%.

@ucsdsummer following increased by 233%.
