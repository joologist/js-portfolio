Dumb Goals

Dumb Goals is an alternative goal-setting system that harnesses the power of the everyday. Set 3 goals at a time— smaller, more approachable milestones that work towards your long-term dreams.

ROLE Content Strategy, Design, Front-End Dev

PARTNER Daniel Huang

TIMEFRAME April - July 2020

TOOLS Figma, React Native, Procreate, Trello

Background

This January, my friends and I set out to make simpler New Year's Resolutions. We each gave ourselves 3 basic goals, & for the first time, I completed my resolutions!



After coming home early from college, my friend Daniel Huang and I turned our unconventional goal-setting system into an app for everyday use.

OBJECTIVES

Make an app that helps users:

1) Set simple goals
2) Easily track progress
3) Stay consistent

Ideating

We explored a wide range of features, considering a social aspect where users could add friends and see their achievements. In the end, we determined that our priority was simplicity and allowing our users to be motivated by their own progress.

Prototyping

To prototype, we used Figma to make low and high fidelity mockups, playing around with a few different visual identities. We wanted a bright, playful brand that was vibrant without being childish, and after plenty of bad ideas, settled on a garden theme that encouraged growth.







Actually Doing It!

As we began to build our app, Daniel took on the heavy programming while I worked on illustrating, writing content, and developing screens using React Native.

The content you see below was written and drawn by me and made into a reality by Daniel. If you'd like to see more Dumb Goals illustrations, more of my work is under Graphic Design!
